## DriveOut Analytics Dashboard: Multi-Page Visual Breakdown

This project features a comprehensive visual analytics platform built using the DriveOut ride company dataset. The architecture translates raw transactional data across Lagos ride operations into high-impact, executive-level business intelligence.

### PAGE 1: Pricing Dynamics & Demographic Insights
The first phase focuses on the foundational relationship between core pricing metrics, regional distribution, and ride consumer demographics.

* **Executive Summary KPI Metrics:** Houses central performance figures detailing total aggregate distance metrics and pricing yields. 
* **Monthly Revenue Optimization Analysis:** Features a structured matrix mapping out ride durations against financial performance. It explicitly pinpoints **May (the 5th month)** as the peak revenue generation period, achieving a yield of **N6 Million**.
* **Weekend Payment Infrastructure Distribution:** A clear evaluation of user transaction choices during weekends. It demonstrates a heavy reliance on **Cash and E-Cards/Wallets**, noting a structural pivot toward electronic channels over the weekend due to regular financial houses being closed.
* **Demographic Segmentation (Marital Status):** A clean distribution model dissecting total client segments. It shows remarkably balanced market usage across four distinct consumer categories: **Widowed (25.8%), Single (25.8%), Married (24.7%), and Divorced (23.7%)**.
* **Geospatial Coverage Index:** Tracks total ride coverage across critical Lagos pickup locations (including Agege, Alimosho, and Surulere), isolating specific spikes in mileage where transit distances are structurally longer.

---

### PAGE 2: Operational Choice & Profit Maximization
The second phase investigates how consumer ride selections under different traffic conditions impact overall profit margins and driver performance ratings.

* **Market Share by Car Type:** Illustrates that the **Premium Model** captures the dominant share of total distance covered at **38%**, followed by Comfort at 25%, Executive at 23%, and Economy at 13%.
* **Vehicle Brand Fleet Performance:** A detailed layout mapping out cumulative distance across primary manufacturers. It highlights **Toyota (37.1K km) and Hyundai (36.6K km)** as the clear fleet leaders due to consumer convenience preferences.
* **Weekend Traffic Density Highlight:** A breakdown showing that **Moderate Traffic** conditions constitute the highest operational environment share at **32.57%**, with Heavy traffic sitting at 28.66% and Very Heavy at 19.14%.
* **Order Integrity Tracking:** A metric analysis tracking order cancellations to measure overall fleet booking retention.

---

### PAGE 3: Qualitative Performance & Financial Close-out
The final phase offers a qualitative and quantitative review of overall system throughput, transactional volumes, and driver ratings.

* **Total Sales Liquidity by Payment Medium:** Isolates exactly which transaction methods favor business infrastructure, revealing **Cash** as a top liquidity driver at **906K**, outperforming direct Card payments (687K), Wallets (614K), and Bank Transfers (569K).
* **Driver Quality Rating Assessment:** Maps out score distributions (clustering heavily around a professional 3.50 to 3.80 scale) and evaluates ratings by customer professional occupation. It shows **Civil Servants** providing the highest overall cumulative assessment score at **218.30**.
* **Hourly Traffic & Duration Highlights:** A dual-axis analysis evaluating vehicle distribution and estimated travel times across a 24-hour cycle, validating specific demand surges.
* **Revenue Yield by Week Day:** Tracks total ride prices across the weekly calendar, identifying **Monday** as the highest total revenue yield day due to the massive surge of the professional working commuter base.
* **Dropoff Destination Revenue Tracking:** A clear regional table mapping total financial yield against dropoff locations, identifying high-yield transit zones such as **Badagry** at **N272,845.57**.
